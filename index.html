About

Data scientist.
Creative thinker.
Problem solver.

I'm Maria — co-founder and CTO of Nami - Data Intelligence, a data consultancy based in Italy. My work sits at the intersection of rigorous data science and creativity: I believe the best analysis is one that uncovers what no one had imagined.

I specialize in Python and R development, machine learning pipelines, data visualization, and translating messy, real-world data into clean, actionable insight; all across business strategy, political analytics, and marketing.

Outside the technical work, I write about data science and its creative side: the aesthetics of visualization, the narrative craft behind good analysis, and the unexpected places where numbers meet culture.
